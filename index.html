Sir Alex Ferguson CBE

The greatest football manager to have ever graced the Earth

Sir Alex Ferguson (SAF) changed the dynamic of football management after his illustrious career came to an end in 2013.
SAF began his managerial career at age 32 with East Stirlingshire in a part time capacity for £40 a week, yet ended his career as the most decorated and successful manager in football history.
Enamoured by most, villified by his rivals - he has undoubtedly left behind a legacy which is unlikely to be surpassed.

List of achievements/honours

  • 13 Premier League titles
  • 5 FA Cups
  • 4 League Cups
  • 2 Champions Leagues
  • 1 European Cup Winners’ Cup
